## Introduction

**OctoFit Tracker** is a fitness tracking application designed for students and groups at Mergington High School. It helps you:

- Track your daily physical activity (running, walking, strength training, and more).
- Compete with classmates through fun, friendly team challenges.
- View a live leaderboard to see how you rank.
- Receive personalized workout suggestions based on your activity history.

The app was built with the goal of making exercise engaging, social, and easy to maintain outside of the classroom.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Open a web browser and navigate to the OctoFit Tracker app URL provided by your teacher or IT department.
2. Click the **Sign Up** button on the home page.
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Username** – Choose a unique display name (no spaces, 3–20 characters).
   - **Email address** – Use your school email.
   - **Password** – Minimum 8 characters; include at least one number or symbol.
4. Click **Create Account**.
5. Check your email for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
6. Log in with your new credentials.

> **Tip:** If you do not receive the verification email within five minutes, check your spam/junk folder.

### Setting Up Your Profile

After your first login you will be guided through a short profile setup:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Display Name** | The name shown to teammates and on the leaderboard. |
| **Grade / Class** | Helps your teacher track class-wide progress. |
| **Fitness Goal** | Choose from *Stay Active*, *Build Strength*, or *Improve Endurance*. |
| **Profile Photo** | Optional — upload a photo or choose an avatar. |

Click **Save Profile** when you are done. You can update these details any time from **Settings → Profile**.

## Core Features

### Activity Logging & Tracking

Logging an activity is the fastest way to earn points and keep your streak alive.

**To log an activity:**

1. Click **Log Activity** in the top navigation bar.
2. Select an activity type from the dropdown:
   - 🏃 Running
   - 🚶 Walking
   - 🏋️ Strength Training
   - 🚴 Cycling
   - 🏊 Swimming
   - ➕ Other (enter a custom name)
3. Enter the **duration** (in minutes).
4. Optionally add a **note** (e.g., "Morning run around the park").
5. Click **Save Activity**.

Your activity will appear in your **Activity Feed** and your points total will update immediately.

**Viewing your history:**

- Navigate to **My Activities** to see a timeline of all your logged sessions.
- Use the date filter to view a specific week or month.
- Each entry shows the activity type, duration, date, and points earned.

---

### Teams

Teams let you collaborate and compete with classmates.

**Joining a team:**

1. Go to **Teams** in the navigation bar.
2. Browse the list of available teams or search by name.
3. Click **Join** on a team card.
4. Some teams may require a join code — ask your team captain for it.

**Creating a team:**

1. Click **Create Team** on the Teams page.
2. Enter a **team name** and optional **description**.
3. Set the team to *Public* (anyone can join) or *Private* (join by code only).
4. Click **Create**. You are automatically the team captain.

**Team dashboard:**

Once you are part of a team you can view:
- Combined team points.
- Individual member contributions.
- Team-wide activity feed.
- Upcoming team challenges set by your teacher.

---

### Leaderboard

The leaderboard shows rankings across all users and teams.

- **Individual leaderboard** — Ranks every student by total points earned in the current month.
- **Team leaderboard** — Ranks teams by their combined monthly points.

Points are updated in real time each time an activity is logged. The leaderboard resets on the first day of each month, so everyone gets a fresh start.

> **How points are calculated:**
>
> | Activity | Points per minute |
> |----------|------------------|
> | Running | 3 |
> | Cycling | 2 |
> | Swimming | 3 |
> | Walking | 1 |
> | Strength Training | 2 |
> | Other | 1 |

### Personalized Workout Suggestions

OctoFit Tracker analyzes your recent activity history and generates workout suggestions tailored to your fitness goal.

- Suggestions appear on your **Dashboard** under *Today's Recommendation*.
- Each suggestion includes the activity type, recommended duration, and a short description of its benefits.
- Click **Log This Workout** to instantly create an activity entry from a suggestion.

Suggestions refresh daily. The more activities you log, the more accurate your recommendations become.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I edit or delete an activity I logged by mistake?**  
A: Yes. Go to **My Activities**, find the entry, and click the **⋮** (three-dot) menu next to it. Select **Edit** or **Delete**.

**Q: What happens if I forget my password?**  
A: Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page and enter your school email. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes.

**Q: Can I be on more than one team?**  
A: Yes. You can join multiple teams, but your points always appear under your own account regardless of how many teams you belong to.

**Q: Why don't I see my points update on the leaderboard right away?**  
A: Points are updated in real time. Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, please contact your IT department.

**Q: Is my personal data private?**  
A: Only your display name and team information are visible to other students. Your email address and detailed activity notes are never shared publicly.

**Q: How do I deactivate my account?**  
A: Contact your teacher or IT administrator. Students cannot self-deactivate accounts to prevent accidental data loss.
